osu! Hybrid Leaderboard

This leaderboard attempts to score (lower is better) the top 10k Ranked Play players based on their PP rank, Elo rank (Ranked Play rank), and participation in tournaments using BWS, with a skew towards Elo. Players without an Elo rank or without a PP rank do not show up on this leaderboard.

Show more

Formula used for this leaderboard:

score = 0.35 × ( pp_rank ^ ( 0.9937 ^ ( badges ^ 2 ) ) ) + 0.65 × elo_rank

The 0.35 / 0.65 split makes the result lean toward live matchmaking Elo, while a strong PP rank still lifts a player. On top of that, badge-weighted seeding (BWS) rewards tournament players by pulling their PP rank toward #1 — only the PP side is adjusted; Elo is left untouched. The more tournament badges a player has, the bigger the lift, so a decorated player isn't dragged down by a deep PP rank. Only badges that link to a tournament forum thread or a wiki Tournaments page are counted. The BWS column is that badge-adjusted PP rank that actually feeds the score. The vs PP column shows how many places a player gained (green ▴) or lost (red ▾) compared with their PP rank alone. Click any column header to sort.

Full source code, the exact badge rules and the scraper: github.com/Acnuma/osu-hybrid-rank-system
